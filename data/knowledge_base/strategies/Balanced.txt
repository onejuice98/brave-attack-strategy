### Strategy Type: Balanced (General Combat Maintenance)

## Situational Awareness
- When battles are expected to last long
- When enemy attack and defense are average
- When allied HP and skill resources are sufficient

## Action Guidelines
- Maintain movements based on unit roles
- Tank takes front position and draws aggro
- DPS units stay behind the tank and deal damage
- Healer stays within range of tank and DPS
- Use skills mid-battle while monitoring cooldowns
